# Debt Trap: Maldives Economic Crisis Analysis

Data-driven analysis of structural fiscal crisis using verified Maldives Monetary Authority (MMA) data, IMF assessments, and Moody's credit analysis.

## Key Findings

- **Persistent fiscal deficits:** 11–13% of GDP (2020–2025)
- **Debt doubled:** 42.7B → 95.8B MVR in five years
- **Revenue collapse:** 2.7% growth vs 5% GDP growth
- **Refinancing crisis:** 52.3B MVR in short-term T-bills requiring monthly rollover
- **External spike:** $600M (2025) and $1B (2026) debt service obligations

- **2025–2026:** Debt service spike and refinancing pressure
- **Mid-2026:** Credit rating downgrade triggers
- **Late 2026:** Market financing dries up
